BEAUTIFUL OUTDOOR LIVING SPACES

Transforming outdoor spaces into livable islands of refuge is the latest trend for homeowners looking to expand their living area and improve the value of their home. From patios and porches to complete outdoor living areas, Apex Builders can expand your family's living space into the great outdoors in so many beautiful ways!

PATIOS AND
PORCHES

Apex Builders can transform your outdoor area for a variety of activities. Don't think of a deck as a single, flat space. Instead, our designers can create separate spaces using various techniques: They build multiple levels connected by stairs, they alternate the type or color of decking material in different areas, and they install structures, such as pergolas, built-in seating, and planter boxes.
