MAKE YOUR BATHROOM A DREAM

As any real estate professional will tell you, great kitchens and bathrooms sell houses. In other words, remodeling your bathroom isn't just a treat for you and your family, it's a sound investment in your home! So whether you are simply replacing fixtures or considering a dream bathroom for your primary suite, make sure it's done right.

As the largest and most respected bathroom remodeler in the Dayton area, Apex Builders' over 40 years of expertise assures the kind of bathroom design and quality renovation you insist upon for your family and your home.

REIMAGINE YOUR BATHROOM EXPERIENCE

BEAUTIFUL AWARD-WINNING DESIGNS

Apex Builders experts work with you to integrate your style, personality, and requirements into world-class bathroom designs that will not only meet, but exceed your expectations.

INVITING, COZY SPACES

Our designers work hard to maximize the utility of a bathroom's space so you've got room for al of the fixtures with enough space to relax and rejuvenate.

THOUGHTFUL FUNCTIONALITY

Bathrooms come in a variety of purposes, from Primary Suites to small guest powder rooms. Our designers ensure the layout and features are perfectly suited to your home's needs for family and guests.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION WITH THE HOME

During your bathroom remodeling process, Apex Builders contractors explain each step along the way, and they strive to keep household interruptions to a minimum by smart planning and direct communication with you.
